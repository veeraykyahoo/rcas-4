MOBILE SUPPORT

Our mobile support provides an excellent physical presence of security when one is needed.
Royal Concierge and Security provides an excellent mobile patrol team. With a lot of experience in the field our team is flexible, visible and provides a lost effective solution to properties which have little to no security as well as assisting site whom have coverage already.

Mobile Patrol is effective by actively patrolling properties at rando times to ensure properties are safe. Before leaving properties, our mobile team goes through our basic security inspection to ensure the safety and security of property while documenting details of the site visit.
